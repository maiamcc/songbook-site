	Oh let me be more like the lily;
Let me grow in waters clear.
	Oh let me be more like the lily;
Show me how my roots grow here.

Let me bloom in the cool of dawn.
Hold me close before I'm gone.

Let me close in the heat of day.
Keep me safe and let me stay

Let me glow in the pale moonlight.
Send me safe into the night. (3x)
