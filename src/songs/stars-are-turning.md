Oh my friends, the **stars** are turning
Over the horizon line.
_(repeat)_

	**Stars** come and go, and come again;
	So remember, all my friends,
	So remember how to **shine**.

Oh my friends, the **moon** is rising,
Warm as spring and bright as snow.
	
	**Moons** come and go...
	...
	So remember how to **grow**.

Oh my friends, the **birds** are waking,
Taking voice and taking wing.
	
	...So remember how to **sing**.

Oh my friends, the birds are waking,
Taking voice and taking wing.
	
	...So remember how to **sing**.

Oh my friends, the **dawn** is breaking,
Quiet in the sky above.

	...So remember how to **love**.

Oh my friends, the **day** is coming;
Sorrows of the night forgive.

	...So remember how to **live**.
