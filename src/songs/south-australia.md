In South Australia I was born!
	Heave away! Haul away!
In South Australia round Cape Horn!
	We're bound for South Australia!

	Heave away, you rolling kings,
	Heave away! Haul away!
	Haul away, you'll hear me sing
	We're bound for South Australia!

As I walked out one morning fair,
'Twas there I met Miss Nancy Blair.

I shook her up, I shook her down,
I shook her round and round the town.

There's just one one thing grieves my mind,
It's to leave Miss Nancy Blair behind.

Aloft, aloft this spar must go,
Around Cape Horn through ice and snow.

And when you sail around Cape Horn,
You'll wish to God you'd never been born!

I wish I was on 'Stralia's strand
With a bottle of whiskey in my hand.
