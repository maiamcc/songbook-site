notes: >-
  Emma writes: An academic paper went viral a few years ago, and the second I saw the title I knew I wanted there to be a song about it. _Cetaceans, sex and sea serpents: an analysis of the Egede accounts of a "most dreadful monster" seen off the coast of Greenland in 1734_ is a very fun read arguing that this often cited sea creature sighting was probably actually whales waving themselves in the air. There are translations from descriptions of the sightings in the paper, and I used many quotes from those passages directly as lyrics in the song.

  I had this concept on my list for awhile but was stuck on how to start. One morning while at camp Dance S'More I woke up with the first phrase of the chorus in my head, and then over the next few days it all came together. It was fun writing this while at camp because a bunch of my friends added ideas and jokes to the song.
