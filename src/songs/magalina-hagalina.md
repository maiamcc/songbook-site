	Magalina Hagalina oaka naka walka talka
	Oaka noka polka was her name (clap clap)!

She had two hairs on the top of her head:
One was _alive_ and the other was _dead_!

She had two eyes set deep in her head:
One was _green_ and the other was _red_!

She had a big mole in the middle of her chin;
She said it was a dimple, but dimples point _in_!

She had two teeth in the middle of her mouth:
One pointed _north_ and the other pointed _south_!

She had two holes in the middle of her noes: 
One was _open_ and the other was _closed_! 

She had two hips like battleships:
One stayed _home_ and the other took _trips_!

She had two feet, size eleven and a half:
One took a _shower_ and the other took a _bath_!

_(Etc.)_

This is the end of Magalina's song...
But you can find her on the internet at hag dot _com_!

_(Repeat chorus ad nauseam, speeding up.)_
