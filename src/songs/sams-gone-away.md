I wish I was a **cabin boy** abord a man o' war;
	Sam's gone away aboard a man o' war!
_(Repeat)_

	Pretty work, brave boys, pretty work I say!
	Sam's gone away aboard a man o' war!
	_(Repeat)_

I wish I was a **sailor**...
...the **coxswain**...
...I was a **midshipman**...
...the **bos'un**...
...the **gunner**...
...the **first mate**...
...the **captain**...
...the **admiral**...
...the **ship's cat**...

I wish I was a **jellyfish** abord a man o' war;
'Cause then I'd be a man o' war aboard a man o' war!"
